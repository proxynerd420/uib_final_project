The Great Hunger...

Vorinclex, the apex predator among Phyrexian Praetors, embodies the insatiable hunger for dominance that defines Phyrexia. Towering and monstrous, Vorinclex wields terrifying power, his form twisted by Phyrexian corruption into a nightmarish amalgamation of flesh and metal. Born from the dark heart of Mirrodin, Vorinclex seeks to spread Phyrexia's influence, crushing all opposition beneath his relentless advance. His mere presence warps the land, draining life and vitality from the world around him. With an army of twisted creations at his command, Vorinclex marches inexorably towards conquest, leaving devastation in his wake. Those who oppose him find themselves facing an enemy unlike any other, a relentless force of nature driven by the unyielding will of Phyrexia.
