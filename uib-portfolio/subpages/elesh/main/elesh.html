Elesh Norn, Grand Cenobite, stands as a paragon of Phyrexian purity and dominance, her name whispered with dread across worlds. Born from the horrors of Phyrexia, she embodies its twisted ideology of perfection through annihilation. As the Praetor of the White faction, she commands legions of zealots and monstrous creations, all dedicated to spreading Phyrexian influence. Her presence is a chilling reminder of the Phyrexian threat, as she seeks to reshape entire planes into extensions of Phyrexia's nightmarish vision. Elesh Norn's influence extends beyond mere military might; she symbolizes the ideological heart of Phyrexia, indoctrinating the weak and eradicating any semblance of opposition. Her cathedral-like form, adorned with macabre symbology, strikes fear into the hearts of those who dare to defy her. Under her command, Phyrexia's march toward domination seems inexorable, leaving only desolation and despair in its wake.
